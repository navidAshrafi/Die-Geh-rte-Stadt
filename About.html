People on the street discussing. Honking cars at the big intersection. The drilling of the new construction site next door. All these are noises that strongly characterise urban life. Noise pollution is therefore becoming a major challenge for life in the city. The project “The Heard City” by Lab:Prepare got to the bottom of this problem. The interdisciplinary group of seven students used an interactive sound map to illustrate noise pollution in Berlin. They put sounds from the everyday life of Berlin students into short snippets on a website to show how much noise students have to deal with in the city.
